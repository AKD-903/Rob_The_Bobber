Instructions Sheet

Game Concept

  • Play as a robber who is infiltrating a house full of dangerous guards and cameras!

    • Core Characters

  • Rob: The main character that you control.
  • Cameras: Rob must avoid being seen by any of these cameras, or Rob dies and the level resets.
  • Guards: Guards are constantly moving around the level; if Rob is seen by any of these moving guards -- Rob dies and the level resets.

    • Instructions

  • Avoid being detected by cameras and guards (stay out of the cameras' line of sight, and avoid being shot by the guard).
  • Guards cannot shoot through / traverse through locked doors. They can and will traverse through open doors, however.
  • Avoid getting caught by an active laser (which is yellow). Lasers will switch from being yellow (active and dangerous) to being green (safe to walk through).
  • Progress down the level (which is organized into different floors) by picking locks to open doors.
  • Escape the level by successfully reaching the exit door (which is clearly indicated).

    • Game Controls

  • Use WASD or arrow keys for basic movement (left, right, up, down).
  • Press 'Q' to begin to get past a locked door.
  • Press 'P' to attempt to pick the lock.
  • Press 'S' to climb down a ladder.
  • Press 'W' to climb up a ladder.
